Solvang

Aug 15, 2022 Food Wine 1 min read 8/10Part of: U.S.A. Road Trip

Overview

A delightfully charming small town we discovered while researching the wine route for our 2-day drive between Los Angeles and San Francisco. Compared to alternatives like Santa Barbara, Santa Maria, and Monterey, accommodation in Solvang is much cheaper and the town itself is a tourist attraction. It ended up being one of the highlights of our entire US trip. If you're into wine, this place offers a more affordable and enjoyable experience than the bigger-name alternatives.

Food & Drink

Our absolute favorite was Solvang Brewing Company. We ate there for both lunch and dinner. Their sausages, onion rings, and fried zucchini and artichoke were genuinely incredible.

Tips & Advice

If you stop by, definitely pop into the gift shop across from the park and say hi to the staff. You'll spot the Turkish Coffee poster at the entrance of the cafe next door, unmissable. Even in a tiny Danish village in the middle of America, we managed to find a Turkish connection. Sometimes I wonder if there's anywhere we haven't spread to.
