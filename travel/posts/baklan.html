Baklan

Dec 30, 2010 City Guide 1 min read 10/10

Overview

According to one legend, the town got its name like this: A group of Yoruk nomads was looking for a place to settle when they arrived at the area where Baklan now stands. One of the elderly women, looking down from the mountaintop at the Baklan plain, said to her grandchild: "Look at the field, dear, isn't it beautiful?" In Turkish, "look at the field" is "bak alana." As everyone kept telling each other "bak alana, bak alana" to show off the scenery, the name stuck. Some of the Yoruks decided to settle down in this area. Over time, "bak alana" was shortened to Baklan.
