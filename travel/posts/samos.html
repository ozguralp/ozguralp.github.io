Samos

Jul 29, 2025 City Guide 1 min read 8/10

Overview

A Greek island we visited for 2 days and 1 night in July. The island is a decent size with quite a lot to see, making it a great location for planning a longer trip.

If you're thinking of it purely as a beach holiday, summer months make sense, but for sightseeing it's a terrible idea because it's scorching hot. The heat was so bad we didn't dare visit any of the historical sites.

Where to Stay

Pythagoreion struck me as the most sensible place to stay. Kokkari isn't bad either but it's smaller. You can do day trips to the beaches from either.

Food & Drink

Portions have shrunk noticeably compared to classic Greek standards, which was a bit of a letdown. Probably picked up bad habits from being so close to Turkey.

Tips & Advice

The island is practically under Turkish occupation. You'll hear Turkish far more than Greek or English. But overall they like Turks here, the negativity you encounter in some other parts of Greece is completely absent on Samos.
