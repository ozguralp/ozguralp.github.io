Melnik

Aug 25, 2023 Food Wine 1 min read 7/10

Overview

The village center is tiny, you can finish your visit with a short stop of about an hour. But the area has a wealth of wineries. While it used to focus more on table wine in the village center, in recent years EU funding has enabled a significant shift toward boutique winemaking. If you're interested in wine, this is definitely worth a stop. With different grape varieties and relatively affordable, high-performing wines, there's something for every palate.

Tips & Advice

Since you're already in the area, the tomb of Baba Vanga is about an hour away, would be a shame to skip it.
