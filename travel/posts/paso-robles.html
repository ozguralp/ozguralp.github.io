Paso Robles

Aug 15, 2022 Food Wine 1 min read 8/10Part of: U.S.A. Road Trip

Overview

The second mini town we chose after Solvang during our research for a 2-day California wine country detour on the road between Los Angeles and San Francisco.

Where to Stay

The reason we picked Paso Robles was actually discovering the Allegretto Vineyard Resort during our research, it looked incredibly appealing. And it truly was an excellent hotel with good wines. Highly recommended.

Food & Drink

If you're into wine, make sure to stop by Arndt Cellars to taste their wines and, if possible, buy some. Including Napa, these were the best wines we tasted in all of America. If you do visit, send our regards to Mr. Arndt, tell him you're the couple who carried his wines back to Turkey and he'll most likely remember us.
